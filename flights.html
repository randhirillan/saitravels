Editor's choice - Top Destinations to Visit

Our planet is a beehive of the spectacular and the majestic! From the tolling of temple bells in Haridwar, India to the coquettish ladyboy shows in Bangkok, Thailand, there is enough in this world to fill up every second of every life! Millions of people travel from one city to another, from one country to another and from one continent to another to fill their lives with varying shades that this planet posseses. Of course, with such abundance comes the problem of plenty and the resulting dilemma it poses for the vacationers. What to seek and what to miss out? Is it the exotic mystique of Cairo or it is the Sydney's unparallel architectural supremacy? Is it matchless cultural downpour of India that sizzles or is it Las Vegas' raunchy night clubs that satiate? Sure, it is the personal inclination that usually determines the place which you would pick the flights tickets for, but then there are holiday destinations that defy the concept of categorisation and are as inviting to one as they are to another!

India

The ravishing parade of incredible delights that are dotted in India is truly unbelievable. India is what all other aspires to be - culturally vibrant, exceedingly diverse, incredibly beautiful and above all, extremely warm towards tourists. A lot of people take flights tickets for travelling to India in an anticipation of finding a land that is drenched in brilliant shades of culture, religion and history. And find they sure do! From the regal forts that inspire awe to stunning temples of ancient ages, India knows how to impress. While the traditional and historical India is well documented and much revered, it is the modern India that is now enticing people to consider tickets options on flights arriving in the ancient nation! Be it the satisfying shopping expedition or a night filled with sensory pleasures, the contemporary India is well-equipped to offer it all!

Thailand - Bangkok

And where do tourists from India go? Yes, you guessed it right, it's Thailand! But it is not just India, people from all over the globe are seeking cheap deals on flights tickets for making a affordable journey to Thailand. In a snapshot of its landscape, Thailand will vividly demonstrate the vast contrasts that ride the place. It is a contrast between the contemporary, all laced with its modern-day finesses and the traditional spectre harping on its cultural might! Bangkok, the capital city, is chaotic, hot, humid, overpowering but the city manages to offer thrills and take the rover on a journey that would most likely be filled with intoxicating dynamism!

Australia - Sydney

Sydney is another big name that manages to impress one and all! Australia's 'the city', Sydney manages to hold on to tourists who are interested in architectural magnificence as effortlessly as it impresses those beach bums who take flights tickets for an Australian tour looking for some old-fashioned seaside fun. This is perhaps one city whose list of tourist attractions is seemingly endless. Sydney Harbour Bridge, The Royal Botanic Gardens, Sydney Opera House, Sydney Aquarium, Oceanworld Manly, Taronga Zoo, Darling Harbour-and it goes on!!
